Our Secret Sauce

How does the Babelus algorithm gather data?

First, our experts label the data and feed the information into the algorithms to generate the first lists of suppliers. Experts then evaluate and validate the results of the ML model.

During the second stage, our algorithms analyze data entered by prospective suppliers. Based on technical parameters, the algorithm looks for the right match based on the customer’s specifications.
